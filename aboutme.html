About us

We are four students from the ninth grade, Akhilesh Balaji, Adhavan Kumar, Dhruv Ramu, and Nachiket Kulkarni. All four of us share a passion for programming, and this extension brings that out. Our extension is called Sched, and there are a number of reasons as to why this extension should be used. Given the current situation the average time spent in front of a screen by a student, teacher, or anyone, has significantly increased. Our extension enables users to be more organised. This extension essentially helps people plan their day; it helps people come up with a unique schedule to optamize their productiviy for a given day. People spend thier day working both online and offline, this extension assists its users to plan their day, whilst calculating the time you spent online, the most frequently visited applications hosted in the worldwide web, and so forth. In the end, the user would be able to see how much time they spent online, and the extension also tells the user whether the time spent online is healthy or not, and based on that also alters their schedule. There are scheduled breaks as well to reduce eye strain. This extension is filled with unique feratures that makes the user's experiance of this app much better, helps users reduce eye strain, and maximises their productivity.
